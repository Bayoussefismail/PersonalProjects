INTERIOR DESIGN

CLASSARCH is a Los Angeles based studio practice focused

on classic design, interios and landscapes from our

inception in 2020, we have delivered elegant private and

buisiness environements that are stimulating to occupy and

fundamental to their surroundings.


As a full service firm, CLASSARCH is present on projects from

start to finish, ensuring that the ideas conceived in the

beginning with clients are manifest at the end. We achieve

this throught collaboration with all members of the

project team including consultants, contractors

trades and artisans.
